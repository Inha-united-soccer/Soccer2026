About Inha-United

Inha-United@Soccer is a collaborative team formed by four major robotics and artificial intelligence laboratories at Inha University, united by a shared goal of advancing autonomous humanoid soccer robotics.

We have also participated in multiple international competitions listed in Table 1 as opportunities to validate our technical skills and acquire practical insights, and have received the awards indicated.

Inha-United@Soccer focuses on the development of a fully integrated robotic system, in which perception, decision-making, and control are closely coupled. With an emphasis on camera-based visual perception, we design object detection and situational awareness algorithms that operate robustly under real match conditions, alongside strategic decision-making frameworks and control architectures that enable coordinated team behaviors.

Ultimately, Inha-United@Soccer aims to push the boundaries of autonomous robotic systems and contribute to the advancement of intelligent robotics.
